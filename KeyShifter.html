Format Rules

Labels

Start each song with a title, then use these labels to mark the song sections.

K :
Song Key
I :
Intro
V :
Verse
P :
Pre-chorus
C :
Chorus
B :
Bridge
In:
Instrumental
O :
Outro
Tr:
Transition

Timing

Put timing symbols between chords to show how long to stay on each chord.

A ' B
1 count
A , B
2 counts
A - B
4 counts
A … B
8 counts

Timing symbols can combine:

A ,' B
3 counts
A -, B
6 counts

Example

Keep spaces between chords and timing symbols so the chart stays easy to read.

TITLE

K : A

V : D - E - D - E -
    D - E , A/C# , D , E , A -

C : A … E … D - E - A …

Slash Chords

Use normal slash chords. The chord before the slash is the main chord, and the note after the slash is the bass note.

A/C#
A main chord, C# bass note
D/F#
D main chord, F# bass note
